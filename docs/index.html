Privacy Policy

Puffer Pop · Digital Diamonds

1. Introduction

Puffer Pop is a free mobile game. This policy explains how we handle your information. We believe privacy should be simple — so we've written this in plain language, not legal jargon.

2. Data we collect

The game itself collects no personal data. Specifically:

  • No account, login or registration is required to play
  • We do not collect your name, email address or location
  • We do not store any information about you on our servers
  • High scores are saved locally on your device only

3. Advertising (AdMob) Google

Puffer Pop shows ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to show you relevant ads. This is handled entirely by Google — we never see or store this data ourselves.

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy .

You can opt out of personalised ads at any time:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → disable for Puffer Pop
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation

We do not sell any personal data to third parties.

4. Analytics Google

Puffer Pop uses Google Analytics to help us understand how players interact with the game — for example, how many sessions are played, which levels are reached and where players drop off.

This data is:

  • Collected anonymously and aggregated — we never see individual user data
  • Used only to improve gameplay and fix issues
  • Never used for advertising targeting
  • Never sold to third parties

For more information, see Google Analytics Privacy Policy . You can opt out of Google Analytics by using your device's "Limit Ad Tracking" (iOS) or "Opt out of Ads Personalisation" (Android) setting.

5. Children

Puffer Pop is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

Our AdMob integration can be configured for child-directed treatment in compliance with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act).

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date at the bottom. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Continued use of the game after changes means you accept the updated policy.
